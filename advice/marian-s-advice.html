Impostor syndrome

This is very common in newbies the famous "I'm not prepared to do those tasks" or "how could i dare to charge that much for a task if I'm only a beginner". Well NEWSFLASH, your time is also valuable and you deserve to the compensated for that.

When are you going to feal comfortable or "good enough" to start charging for your work/time? When are you going to be confident that you are qualified for a job? Nobody knows everything, you are always in constant learnig, take a chance, be confident and after you finish this course you'll have the skills adecuate to apply.

Be perseverant, have faith in you and the skills you have, be constant with your learning. Organize your day to practice/learn at least one hour a day. You'll be surprise of how fast you are advancing and what an awesome programmer you have become.
